Project Overview

IntelliFlow is a production-grade water pump automation system designed for critical water infrastructure where reliability is non-negotiable. It eliminates the failure modes of float switches and timer-based controllers by enforcing deterministic, sensor-verified operation.

The system protects pumps from dry-running, prevents tank overflow, handles intermittent water sources, and rejects false triggers caused by turbulence or debris. It operates fully autonomously without cloud dependency and is suitable for 24/7 deployment in municipal, agricultural, and industrial applications.

System Architecture

The architecture is centered on an ESP32 microcontroller and follows a strict separation of concerns. Conductivity-based sensing feeds a deterministic finite state machine, which drives an opto-isolated pump relay stage.

Hardware Components

The system uses industrial-grade stainless steel electrodes for reliable long-term operation, with proper signal conditioning and isolation for safe pump control.

Optimized GPIO selection with proper signal conditioning ensures reliable operation in electrically noisy pump environments.

Operation States & Logic Flow

Professional calibration ensures accurate water detection with appropriate thresholds and timing adjustments for different water conductivity levels.

Control Logic & Finite State Machine

Deterministic four-state machine ensures predictable behavior and prevents pump damage through strict verification and timeout mechanisms.

  1. STATE_WAITING_FOR_WATER - Initial state monitoring for water presence
  2. STATE_STARTUP_STABILIZATION - 3-minute water stability verification
  3. STATE_PUMP_RUNNING - Active pumping with continuous monitoring
  4. STATE_FAILURE_COOLDOWN - 20-minute recovery after failures

Safety & Protection Mechanisms

  • Two-stage dry-run protection (Electrode 1 + Electrode 2)
  • Tank overflow prevention (Electrode 3 monitoring)
  • Mandatory cooldown after failures (20-minute timeout)
  • Reset cause diagnostics and error reporting
  • Graceful recovery from power failures

Firmware Architecture

Modular, offline-first firmware with deterministic behavior and safety isolation. Built around a finite state machine with millis()-based non-blocking timing for 24/7 operation.

Installation & Field Deployment

IP66 enclosure with opto-isolated pump control and strict electrode placement guidelines. Proper grounding and electrical safety measures for industrial environments.

Performance Outcomes & Reliability
