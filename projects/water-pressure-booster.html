ESP32 Automatic Inline Water Pressure Booster System

Flow-based intelligent booster pump controller designed for reliable, energy-efficient water pressure enhancement with zero manual intervention.

Project Overview

This project addresses the critical problem of inconsistent water pressure in residential and agricultural systems by implementing a smart, flow-activated booster pump controller that eliminates energy waste, prevents pump damage, and operates autonomously.

Unlike conventional pressure-switch systems that cycle unnecessarily, this ESP32-based controller activates the pump only when genuine water flow is detected, ensuring efficient operation and extended equipment lifespan.

System Architecture & Subsystems

Flow Detection Subsystem

A YF-S201 Hall-effect flow sensor provides real-time water flow monitoring, generating pulses that are captured via hardware interrupt on the ESP32. Software debouncing and sampling ensure accurate flow measurement.

Control & Logic Subsystem

The ESP32 executes a deterministic state machine using non-blocking, millis()-based timing. All thresholds and timing parameters are configurable for different applications.

Power Switching Subsystem

A Songle SRD-05VDC-SL-C relay module provides opto-isolated switching between the low-voltage control electronics and the high-voltage AC booster pump, ensuring electrical safety and reliability.

User Feedback Subsystem

Dual-color LED status indication provides immediate visual feedback on system state without requiring external monitoring equipment.

Power & Wiring System

Clean power distribution with separate circuits for logic and pump control, ensuring stable ESP32 operation even during pump switching events.

Operation Sequence & State Machine

The controller follows a precise operational sequence that validates genuine water demand before pump activation, with built-in timing safeguards to prevent short-cycling and equipment damage.

Safety & Protection Mechanisms

Multiple protection layers ensure safe operation, prevent equipment damage, and extend system lifespan through intelligent control logic.

Configuration & Application Tuning

The system is highly configurable for different applications through simple constant adjustments in the firmware.

Real-World Applications

The system is versatile enough to address various water pressure challenges across residential, agricultural, and commercial settings.

Engineering Challenges & Solutions

  • False activation from water hammer: Solved using 2-second sampling window and 3-pulse minimum threshold.
  • Pump short-cycling: Eliminated through 10-second minimum run time and 5-second minimum off time enforcement.
  • Dry running damage: Prevented by requiring confirmed flow before and during pump operation.
