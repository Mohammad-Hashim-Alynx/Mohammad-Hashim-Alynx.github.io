Programmable Automatic Egg Incubating Machine

Fully automated egg incubation system designed to maintain stable environmental conditions over a complete 28-day incubation cycle.

Problem Statement

Traditional manual egg incubation is highly sensitive to temperature fluctuation, humidity imbalance, inconsistent egg turning, and power outages, often leading to low hatch rates and high chick mortality.

System Overview

The system implements closed-loop control of temperature, humidity, ventilation, and egg turning using discrete controllers and electromechanical actuators, ensuring consistent incubation conditions without human intervention.

Key Features

  • Closed-loop temperature control using W1209 controller
  • Automated egg turning using AC swing motor mechanism
  • Forced-air ventilation for uniform heat distribution

  • Unattended operation throughout incubation cycle

Results & Outcomes

The system achieved stable incubation conditions throughout the full incubation period, eliminating manual intervention and significantly improving hatch consistency and survival rates.
