Engineering Profile

I am a Mechanical Engineer specializing in automation, control systems, and machine-level engineering for real-world utility and industrial applications. I focus on designing systems that operate reliably in unattended field conditions, where safety, determinism, and fault tolerance are critical.

My work spans pumps, heating systems, water treatment, and embedded control hardware, covering the full lifecycle from requirements analysis and control logic design to commissioning and field deployment.

Engineering Philosophy

I prioritize deterministic behavior, hardware safety interlocks, and non-blocking firmware architectures over cloud dependency or fragile abstractions. Every system is designed to fail safely, recover autonomously, and operate continuously with minimal human intervention.
